I am a PhD candidate at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), supervised by Prof. [Xiaojuan Ma](https://www.cse.ust.hk/~mxj/) since 2024.

Prior to joining HKUST, I earned my Master’s degree (2024) from [DALAB](http://dalab.se.sjtu.edu.cn/www/home/) at Shanghai Jiao Tong University under the supervision of Prof. [Xubo Yang](http://dalab.se.sjtu.edu.cn/www/home/?page_id=143). I also completed my Bachelor’s degree at Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2021.

My research interests lie at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Virtual Reality (VR), and Augmented Reality (AR), with a specific focus on understanding user perception in immersive environments.
